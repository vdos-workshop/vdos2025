Bio:

Dr. Rebecca Racz (PharmD, University of Michigan) brings a strong background in pharmacology and regulatory science to our workshop. After earning her Doctor of Pharmacy degree from the University of Michigan, she embarked on a career that spans clinical pharmacokinetics, adverse‐event analysis, and federal regulatory work. Dr. Racz has contributed to research at the U.S. Food and Drug Administration, specializing in drug safety and vaccine­related outcomes. Her commitment to advancing medication safety and evidence-based decision-making ensures she brings valuable insight and experience to our participants.
